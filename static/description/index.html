SAMPLE SUBMISSION APP

By Nizam

This module will enables efficient management of sample submissions, linking customers to submission forms and tracking related materials as products.

Description with screenshots

To enable XLSX reporting, please install the 'report_xlsx' module after installing this module.

Next, navigate to the side menu and select the 'Sample Submission' app to access its features.

Click the New button located at the top-left corner to create a new sample submission.

Upon creating a new sample submission, the Sample Submission Form will appear, and the status will automatically update from 'Pending' to 'Doing'.

Initiate material records creation by clicking the Action button and selecting Create Material Records from the dropdown menu.

Upon selecting 'Create Material Records', a wizard will popup. Fill in the required fields with appropriate data and click the Create button to generate material records linked to the corresponding sample submission.

The newly created material record will appear on the Materials page. From here, you can Delete the record, but editing or adding new records is only possible through the Create Material Records wizard.

Create an invoice for the sample submission by selecting Create Invoice from the Action dropdown.

A confirmation popup wizard will appear. Click OK to confirm and create the invoice.

After creating the invoice, a button link will appear, allowing you to View Invoice

Clicking the Invoices button link will redirect you to the Created Invoice Tree View, displaying the generated invoice.

Clicking the generated invoice will display details of the corresponding sample submission, including a Sample Submission button link to navigate to the responsible sample submission form. Click Confirm to proceed.

After clicking Confirm, click Register Payment to proceed with payment registration.

A popup wizard will appear. Enter the payment amount (note: amount is less than the invoiced amount, for testing purpose). The payment difference will be displayed. Click Create Payment to proceed.

Click the Sample Submission button link to navigate to the sample submission details.

Click Sample Submission in the breadcrumb navigation path.

The newly created sample submission will appear in the Tree View, displaying fields such as Collected Payment, Balance, Sum of Cost etc...
These fields are updated after completing the invoice payment process.

Click the Report button located on the top navigation bar.

A popup wizard appeared with Date From and Date To for filtering the report. You can see two buttons for two options: one for printing the PDF Report and another for exporting the Excel Report.

Alternatively, filter reports by clicking the Filter Invoice option from the Action menu dropdown list in any Sample Submission form.

Print PDF and Excel reports by clicking the Print button located at the top-left corner of any corresponding Sample Submission form.

Sample Submission Access Privileges

Configure access privileges under Sample Submission category in Settings:
Access Level Permissions
Administration
  • Access all records
  • Edit records
  • Delete records
  • Create new records
All Documents
  • Access all records
  • Edit records
  • Create new records
  • No delete permission
Own Documents
  • Access own records only
  • Edit own records
  • Create new records
  • No delete permission
